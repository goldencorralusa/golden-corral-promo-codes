Golden Corral Military Discount

Golden Corral has one of the most recognized military appreciation programs in the American restaurant industry. The Golden Corral military discount reflects a genuine commitment to service members that goes back more than two decades. In 2026, most locations offer a 10% to 15% discount for active duty military, retirees, and veterans throughout the year, not just during special events.

The annual Military Appreciation Night is the crown jewel of this program. Every Veterans Day on November 11, Golden Corral invites all service members, past or current, for a free dinner buffet from 5 PM until closing time. The restaurant has been running this tradition for over 20 years and has served millions of free meals through it. In practice, many veterans miss this event simply because they did not know it was happening.

Golden Corral does not run large national ad campaigns for it every year. The smarter move is to set a calendar reminder for early November and call your local restaurant a week before Veterans Day to confirm their participation. To claim your discount at any time, bring a valid Military ID, VA card, or state ID with a veteran designation. Some locations use SheerID for online verification.

Golden Corral Kids Eat Free Offers

For families with young children, Golden Corral already offers one of the better pricing structures in the buffet category. The base pricing is tiered by age, which means you never pay adult rates for a toddler who barely finishes a plate of mac and cheese. That said, knowing exactly what qualifies and when saves you from wrong assumptions before you visit.

Children aged 3 and under eat free at Golden Corral every day with the purchase of one adult buffet, and this is limited to two free toddlers per paying adult. A common mistake parents make is assuming this applies without restrictions or that it extends to older kids automatically. It does not. For older children, some locations host Kids Eat Free nights, usually on select weekdays, where kids can eat free when accompanied by a paying adult.

These promotions are not available nationwide since each Golden Corral is independently operated. On days where kids can eat free, the offer typically applies up to age 12, but terms vary. Most locations announce Kids Night specials on their local Facebook pages, in-store posters, or through the Good as Gold email club. A quick call to your nearest location before you go is the most reliable way to confirm what is running that week.
